For Parents

Parents, at SNC we use adventure, learning and kindness all in a natural setting to promote each child's personal development. Campers tell us that our nurturing atmosphere gives them confidence to make friends easily.

Yet most rewarding is hearing that SNC allows each camper to truly be themselves. When your SNC camper returns home more responsible, resilient and kinder, you will know your decision to send your child to SNC was the right one.
